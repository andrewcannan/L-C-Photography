About Me

Hi, I’m Lloyd Chidgey – thank you so much for checking out my site. I am a full-time photographer with over fifteen years of experience. I love to work with people; from beautiful newborn babies through to families, from weddings to concerts – you name it, I’ve probably photographed it!

Photography is my passion! When my brother gifted me my first DSLR I fell in love! Capturing the amazing people in my life around me, a moment captured in time, was just the best feeling in the world. I haven’t put my camera down since and now I feel so honored to be photographing other people's families or events and helping them document their own memories.

Realising that your photoshoot, wedding or event is a highly personal experience to you, I pride myself in capturing those authentic moments, those precious memories and important milestones. My focus is to create full-of-life, natural imagery which tells your story, in its true form. No awkward, rigid posing, just fun, fresh and natural photography.

If you’re looking for a friendly, creative photographer who will value your memories as much as you do and will bring an abundance of energy to your wedding, photoshoot or concert. Then you've come to the right place! Check out my Testimonials and Gallery as they are far better at describing my services than I am and if you feel as though I'm the photographer for you , then please do get in touch with me here or via my Facebook or Instagram or Twitter.
